Hobbies:

Because of this pandemic, I have found lots of new hobbies, and reintroduced myself to my old forgotten ones.

I love to paint. That will always be one of my hobbies, I'm sure! I have a very big basket full of painting supplies such as paintbrushes, paint, watercolor and acrilic pads of paper to paint on, and many other items. I make painting a two hour project, as I watch (and pause) YouTube painting tutorials.

Adding to the topic of arts and crafts, I am currently creating a mini model of a home. It is so fun, but it requires a lot of patience as you will have to wait for glue to dry and you will have to do things slowly. It is not a project which you glue ready-made objects down. You have to re-create each candle,table,chandelier,sofa, and many other items with wires,cloth,tiny wood boards and paper.

I play the piano. When I have nothing else to do or I need to calm down, I play the very calming instrument. I have been playing the piano for three years, and I took lessons from a studio not far from where I live. I stopped taking piano lessons last summer, when I thought that I would rejoin, but there wasn't enough time to do that. I have been re-teaching the lessons in my workbook to myself so I can still get better even if I don't take any lessons.

I have just started a very big project, which is to clean my room. My parents and I have decided to bring the stuff I have on the ground up, meaning to put them away and to create more storage spaces. We plan on getting a chest and armoire so my clothes can stay in one place and a bookshelve so we can put my many books in there and also for the extra storage space.
