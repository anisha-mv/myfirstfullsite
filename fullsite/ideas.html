Ideas:

This page has ideas for what to do when you are bored and just in general!

  • Keep up with school work so when you go back you can still recall lessons. I recomend the Summer Bridges Activity book
  • Help around the house!It takes a lot less screaming from your parents and it spends time!
  • Limit Screen Time. If you are doing online schooling, there is no need to worsen your eyesight by adding to the too-much-sceen-time you already have
  • Clean around the house!!It takes a huge chunk of boredom out of your day and you feel good afterword too.
  • Start a project. You wiil have something to look foreword to and it will cure boredom. Exaples of projects are painting, programming something for fun, fun science experiments, etc.
  • Learn a new language!It's so fun!
  • Talk to your parents about your family history.It will really fasinate you.
  • Read!Open yourself to a world of possibilities like fantasy. Settle somthing you can rely on like non-fiction. Make a connection with realistic fiction books. Understand others through biographies.
  • Play outside.Vitamin D is very good for you and you need to socialize with others. It is what define humans!*Be sure to maintain at least 6 feet in social distance and wear a face mask*
  • Stay in touch with friends!It feels good.
  • Ajust your sleep pattern, School starts soon!
